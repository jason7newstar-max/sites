Al Badawi

⭐ Eater 38 (canonical best NYC)

介绍

This full-service offshoot of Palestinian restaurant Ayat, specializes in family-sized portions of flatbreads, ouzi beef, and its acclaimed mansaf (lamb with fermented yogurt sauce over rice).
